About the Firm

Designed with Purpose.
Built to Last.

Arcscene Architects (K) Ltd is a BORAQs-registered architectural firm based in Nairobi, Kenya. Led by Managing Director J. Mwaniki, we deliver design excellence across residential, commercial, institutional, and recreational developments throughout Kenya.

From first sketch to final handover, we manage every phase — architectural design, interior planning, project supervision, and delivery. Our team brings precision, creativity, and a deep understanding of the Kenyan built environment to every commission.
